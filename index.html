The problem

The call every business owner dreads

It usually starts with a customer:

Text message · 9:12 AM

“Hey, I tried to visit your website and it’s showing some weird error.”

By then, it’s already been down for hours — maybe days. Every person who searched for you in that time found a broken page and moved on to a competitor.

Here’s what most business owners don’t know until it’s too late:

Websites don’t maintain themselves.

The software behind your site releases security patches constantly. Skip them, and you’re leaving the door unlocked.

Hackers don’t target big companies — they target easy ones.

Automated bots scan thousands of small business sites a day looking for outdated software. It’s nothing personal. It’s just easy.

This isn’t scare talk — it’s measured.

6,700 new WordPress vulnerabilities logged by security researchers in just six months. Source: Patchstack
4 in 10 of those vulnerabilities were exploitable in real-world attacks. Source: Patchstack
61% of compromised WordPress sites were running outdated software at the time of the attack. Industry analyses
$500–$1,500 what agencies typically charge to recover a hacked site — if your data can be recovered at all. Emergency cleanup

Staying updated isn’t housekeeping. It’s the whole game. And without a recent backup, years of work can vanish permanently.

When it breaks, who do you even call?

If the answer is “the person who built it years ago” or “my nephew,” you don’t have a plan. You have a hope.

What if my website is already broken or hacked?

We can help. We’ll assess it, quote a one-time cleanup, and once it’s healthy, your care plan keeps it that way.

I didn’t build my site with you. Can you still maintain it?

Yes — we take over WordPress sites built by anyone. The first thing we do is a full backup and health check.

What if I want to cancel?

Send one email. No contracts, no cancellation fees, no hard feelings. We’ll even hand over your latest backup on the way out.

What counts as a “website change” on the Peace of Mind plan?

Text edits, photo swaps, updating hours or menus, small layout tweaks — anything reasonable that takes under an hour total per month. Bigger projects (new pages, redesigns, new features) get a separate simple quote first, so there are never surprise charges.

Do I really need this if my site seems fine?

“Seems fine” is how every hacked site looked the day before. The software behind your site needs regular patching whether or not anything looks wrong — that’s what keeps it fine.

What kind of websites do you work with?

We specialize in WordPress, which powers most small business websites. Not sure what yours runs on? Send us the address and we’ll tell you for free.

Why do your new websites come with a care plan?

Because building a website and leaving it unprotected is like handing someone car keys with no oil changes, ever. Most hacked sites are compromised through outdated software — protection isn’t an extra, it’s part of doing the job right.
